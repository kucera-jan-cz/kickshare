Kickshare is community project which helps Kickstarter fans organize their group pledges. The idea is simple - Kickshare works as a platform for finding people who backs the same project as you so that you can form a group and save money on shipping. It does not matter whether you are aiming for multiple 10X+ group pledge or you just want to split shipping fee in two with other backer from your town.
Everybody wins, except postal offices. Group is a form of registered users with one leader (owner of the group) and multiple backers. Leader takes the most responsibility in the group, he organizes the pledge and Kickshare is mainly build for this role. It helps him store information about backers (what they want to pledge, extra add-ons ets) and helps communicate with them. For backers it mainly provides information that such groups exists and is formed. It does not protect you anyhow. Kickshare is build on old fashioned gentlemen's (and gentlewoman's) word. The system encourages flow which minimize risks but does not work as middle man (yet?). Kickshare is open source project with no intentions of profit.

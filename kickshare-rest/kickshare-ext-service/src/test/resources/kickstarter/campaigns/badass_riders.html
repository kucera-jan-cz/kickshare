      * Badass Riders will be produced in English, French, German and Spanish.

    ** All the texts has been translated to english by us for prototyping purposes but they will be rewritten by professional native translators of each language for the final versions.

    The strongest and most violent competition on wheels is about to start. The smell of petrol, blood, kerosene and gunpowder mix in a desert blurred with the scars caused by hundreds of tyres. The pilots howl with euphoria after the starting line while their mechanic frames roar waiting for the starting pistol shot. Missiles, machine guns, blades, explosive spears, antitank mines, the vehicles are ready to depart. Soon they pistons will go off and the sand below the tyres will explode. Badass Riders has just started.

    Badass Riders is a competitive card game for 2 to 6 players who recreate a frenetic race of armed cars in a post-apocalyptic future. Each player chooses a vehicle and a pilot, both with their abilities and unique characteristics wich could be combined to give rise to different strategies. The terrain cards permit you to build the circuit on wich the vehicles will run. You can create or build completely personalised circuits, wich permits you to adjust the complexity and duration of the game by adding or more or less special terrains, collocating traps, sandstorms, snipers, arsenals or even a huge and monstrous sandworm.

    About Koti Games

    Koti Games is a company created by four professionals from the video game industry: Carlos Fabregat, Patricia Ponce, Germán Zamorano and Rubén Segura, each with 8 to 10 years of experience, that are united by their love of board games. The video game industry has given them the necessary skills in game design, illustration, modeling, balancing and testing, that they needed to carry out this project.

    About Pablo Rodríguez

    Pablo is an enthusiast of board games and role-playing games and a designer of great talent. Pablo designed Badass Riders and when he proposed to us to work together to realize this project we do not hesitate for a moment. He has designed an easy-to-learn but incredibly addictive game. We are sure that this will be the first of many projects working together.

    About Märchen Atelier

     

    Heriberto is a talented 3D sculptor from Mexico and founder of Märchen Atelier 3D company. He managed the 3D sculting of several high level games and now he brings us these badass miniatures for our vehicles.

    Risks and challenges

    The main risks in any project of this size are the delays, such as:

    • Delays in the game design: in order to avoid delays in the game design, we have not started the Kickstarter campaign until the game rules, art, design, gameplay balance and testing have been finished. The core game is completely ready for manufacturing.
    • Delays in the manufacture: these delays are not controlled by us, but rather by the factory where the game is to be manufactured. We are working hard to minimize delays by choosing a factory with large experience in boardgame manufacturing rather than a cheaper one. In order to further expedite the process, we have already sent all the graphic files to this factory, where they have been validated. The manufacturing process is ready to start as soon as the Kickstarter campaign ends.
    • Delays in the shipping: as above, the delays in shipping do not depend on us. To minimize shipping delays, we have decided to work with highly-established freight and shipping companies.

    Throughout the campaign and until the last shipment is made, we will continue working to our best in order to achieve the maximum satisfaction from all those who have placed their trust on us.
